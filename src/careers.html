TL;DR

Early-stage role for someone who enjoys mapping business processes, implementing automations, and working directly with clients. You’ll work on-site in Kingston, ON to design, build, and support reliable AI-driven workflows for service-focused teams.

About Flowtica

Flowtica is an AI systems studio that designs, builds, and operates AI assistants and workflow automations for service businesses and operations-heavy teams. We focus on dependable systems, measurable outcomes, and clear handoffs to the teams that use them.

What You’ll Do

  • Map client processes and translate them into automation workflows.
  • Configure and test AI assistants, integrations, and handoffs across phone, chat, and internal tools.
  • Collaborate closely with the founder to scope, deliver, and iterate on client implementations.
  • Create documentation, runbooks, and training materials that keep systems maintainable.
  • Spend time on-site with clients in Kingston to gather feedback and ensure deployments fit real-world operations.

Must-Have Qualifications

  • Comfort translating messy processes into clear, testable steps.
  • Hands-on experience with automation tools or scripting (Zapier, n8n, Make, Python, JavaScript, or similar).
  • Strong written communication for documenting workflows and client updates.
  • Ability to work on-site in Kingston, ON and visit client locations when needed.

Nice-to-Have

  • Familiarity with AI assistant design (LLMs, prompt engineering, retrieval, evaluation).
  • Experience integrating CRMs, phone systems, or helpdesk tools.
  • Comfort testing APIs and using developer tools or Postman.
  • Background in operations, support, or process improvement roles.

What We Offer

  • ~$50,000 base salary plus stock options.
  • Direct mentorship and visibility into how client systems are built.
  • Meaningful ownership over implementations that ship quickly and improve real workflows.
  • A collaborative on-site environment in Kingston with clients nearby.
