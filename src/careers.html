Careers at Flowtica

Flowtica is a Canadian AI consultancy focused on getting real AI and automation into production for businesses. Our work is delivery-first: we ship workflows, assistants, and integrations that run in real environments, not demo sandboxes. Roles are senior-leaning, impact-driven, and client-facing.

What it’s like to work at Flowtica

  • Small senior team that ships production systems for customers.
  • High ownership with autonomy to choose approaches that keep deployments reliable.
  • Direct collaboration with business clients to understand operations and outcomes.
  • Bias toward deploying AI and automation, not research experiments or demos.
  • Clear expectations, accountability, and quick feedback from the founder.
  • Remote-friendly for collaboration, with time on-site when needed for delivery.

Automation Implementation Specialist (Junior–Mid)

Work model: On-site (Kingston, ON) • Engagement: Full-time • Location: Canada

Compensation: ~$50,000 base + stock options

View details

TL;DR

  • Own delivery of production automations with 1–3 years in integrations, internal tools, or technical ops.
  • Comfortable with REST APIs, JSON, and writing small JavaScript/Python helpers.
  • Client-facing and able to translate discovery into scoped plans, then build and iterate.
  • Based on-site in Kingston, ON with clear upside through options and performance raises.

Role overview

You will own end-to-end implementations: run discovery with customers, align technical plans with the founder, build the workflows, and keep them reliable in production.

How you’ll work with clients

  • Lead discovery calls to map workflows, constraints, and success criteria.
  • Document requirements crisply and propose delivery plans that balance speed and stability.
  • Communicate progress, risks, and decisions directly with customers and the founder.

Ownership and delivery

  • Build automations in n8n (or similar), connect systems via REST APIs, and write targeted helpers in JavaScript/Python.
  • Use LLMs pragmatically for parsing and classification when it improves production workflows.
  • Add logging, monitoring, and simple runbooks so deployments are observable and debuggable.
  • Ship iterations based on real usage while keeping systems stable.

You will have close mentorship and review from the founder, but are expected to solve problems independently and keep projects moving.

Must-have qualifications

  • 1–3 years in automation/integration engineering, technical ops/RevOps, internal tools, or software delivery.
  • Practical experience with at least one automation approach (n8n, Zapier, Make/Integromat, or scripting).
  • Confident reading API docs, making authenticated REST requests, and handling JSON payloads.
  • Ability to write basic JavaScript or Python, including data transforms and webhook/event handling.
  • Strong communication: can run client calls, ask precise questions, and write clear updates.

Nice-to-have

  • n8n experience and multi-system workflows (e.g., CRM + email + Slack/SMS).
  • LLM usage for extraction/classification with structured outputs.
  • Basic SQL for logging/analytics and familiarity with tools like Twilio, Slack, SendGrid, HubSpot, or Salesforce.
  • Exposure to B2B ops domains such as property management, staffing, or distribution.

What we offer

  • ~$50,000 base salary depending on experience, plus stock options.
  • Performance-based raises tied to delivery and ownership.
  • On-site collaboration in Kingston, ON with hands-on support from the founder.
  • End-to-end ownership of production implementations and practical AI work.
