About Flowtica

We are Flowtica, an AI systems studio

Flowtica exists to bridge the gap between impressive AI demos and systems that quietly do real work every day. We approach AI like infrastructure: something that should be reliable, observable, and integrated into your existing tools—not a disconnected experiment that lives in a separate tab.

We partner with teams who already feel the pain of manual workflows, scattered knowledge, and constant context switching. Together, we design small, testable projects that earn trust, and then grow them into durable systems over time.

How we work

Phase 1

Discovery & workflow mapping

We start by unpacking how your team actually works today—systems, constraints, and the annoying edge cases that break naive automation.

Phase 2

Prototype in weeks, not months

We design a minimal, testable AI assistant or workflow that plugs into your existing stack. If it doesn't improve something measurable, we don't ship it.

Phase 3

Harden & scale

We add monitoring, fallbacks, guardrails, and performance tuning so your AI system behaves like real infrastructure, not a toy demo.

Principles

Principle 1

Own your data

Your data stays under your control. We prefer architectures where you can self-host or use your own cloud whenever possible.

Principle 2

Don't fight your stack

We integrate with what you already use—CRMs, ticketing systems, databases—instead of forcing new tools everywhere.

Principle 3

Small bets, real impact

We favour small projects with clear value over massive "AI transformation" initiatives that never launch.
